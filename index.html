Happy New Year!

Welcome to 2014 everyone! Although, I don't really consider it the new year until I stop writing the year wrong.. So I'll accept the new year in about three months or so. As some of you may have noticed, I haven't been updating very often recently. I added comments!

So, as I mentioned in the last update, I really wanted to find an easy way to add comments to my page. Thanks to the information from a friend of mine, the comments section is now active! You will notice that I have replaced the "Links" button with the "Comments" button. There you will find a neat little thing that will allow you to post comments to my site! It's really simple, all you have to do is log in with your Facebook, Twitter, or Google+ account and then you are free to leave a comment. Your feedback is greatly appreciated and I will probably expand the comments section so that it's not just one place on the entire site to comment. But for now, this will work quite nicely for what I need it to do. So, again, please let me know what you think and any feedback is greatly appreciated!
