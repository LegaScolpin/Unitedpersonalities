Final Fantasy X

Final Fantasy X is one of a series of fourteen games and I do believe Square Enix is still making more. I may be a little bias, since I’m a girl and I haven’t played very many games but I have to say Final fantasy X has the best story line ever with action, fantasy, adventures, twists and little bit of romance! I love, love, love it!

Minecraft

If you haven't heard of Minecraft then you've been living under a rock, because Minecraft has worked it's way into almost every part of internet culture. Minecraft is a sandbox creative game whose looks can be a little deceiving. If there was ever a game that fit the phrase "don't judge a book by it's cover", this is that game.

Dota 2

You could call Dota 2 the ancestor of the original MOBA game, Dota. When DotA (Defence of the Ancients) was released as a mod in WarCraft 3 multiplayer, it became the largest sensation to hit the multiplayer online experience in a long time. Dota 2 captures that experience and expands on it.
